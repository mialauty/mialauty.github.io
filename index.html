Happy anniversary!

For eight years, I've been in love with a girl, who falls asleep like a narcoleptic, and who subconsciously hums random tunes at random times in random places, and who cries when she laughs and laughs when she cries and laughs all the time (so she's always in a superposition of all states). This girl is in love with me too, and also salmon sushi, and ballet, and Grey's Anatomy, and Gong Yoo.

This girl is my light, my hope, my whole world. She is the engine oil keeping my pistons torquing, the air-conditioning keeping me comfortable when I run hot, the crossword puzzle keeping my brain two steps ahead of alzheimers, and the sous-vide machine making sure I come out cooked all the way through.

The years I have spent with her have been nothing short of magical. Life before her was like a Starbucks Verona: dark and full of terrors. Life is now a Hacienda La Esmeralda Gesha: bright, sweet, juicy, and complex (and expensive)!

July 2011

Eight years ago, we started with a kiss, or two.

One, with Neon and Stop This Train and Gravity playing in the background, laying on a shallow mattress on the floor of a soon to be torn down apartment.

And then, a kiss of the chocolate variety, cheesily hidden and then buried and then (painfully) dug up, while eating dessert, after a purposefully event-less date.

Both times:

surprising

yet also:

earnestly craved.

Just like then, I have something of a surprise:

I've been sneaking around the past few months on clandestine missions, scouting would-be locations...

... locations, where I have secretly been luring you to.

...

all, that I may show how much I love you.

and because the eight years were long,
but not long enough.
